Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for re-reviewing MenuPic AI.

Guideline 5.1.1(v) update implemented:
- Users can access core menu scanning without creating an account.
- Sign-in is optional and used for account portability/recovery.

How to test without registration:
1. Launch the app.
2. On the welcome screen, tap Continue as Guest.
3. You will be taken directly to the Scan tab.
4. Point camera at a menu and tap detected dish text to run analysis.

Account behavior:
- Sign-in with Apple/Google is optional.
- Users may sign in later to sync purchases across devices.
- Guest and signed-in scan usage is tracked server-side.

If anything is unclear, please let us know and we can provide a short video walkthrough.

Thank you.
